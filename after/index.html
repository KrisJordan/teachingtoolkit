    After the Semester¶

    The semester is winding down—congratulations! This section helps you wrap up effectively and set yourself up for future success.

    In This Section¶

    ### End of Semester Checklist Wrap up tasks and deadlines

    ### Reflecting & Iterating Turn this semester's lessons into next semester's improvements

    End of Semester Timeline¶

    Last Week of Classes¶

    • Communicate final exam details
    • Provide study guidance and review materials
    • Complete any remaining grading from the semester
    • Send course evaluation reminders

    Finals Week¶

    • Administer final exam
    • Hold extended office hours for questions
    • Begin final grading

    After Finals¶

    • Complete all grading by the deadline
    • Submit grades through ConnectCarolina
    • Review course evaluations when available
    • Document what worked and what to change

    Key Deadlines¶

    Refer to the Registrar's Academic Calendar for official grade submission deadlines.
