# AI Office Hours Prep

**Time savings: ~15-20 minutes per session**

Anticipate student questions and prepare helpful explanations before office hours by using AI to analyze common points of confusion.

!!! info "Coming Soon"
    This page is under development. Check back soon for the complete guide.

## Quick Overview

Use AI to:

1. **Identify likely questions** based on recent course content
2. **Generate multiple explanations** of difficult concepts
3. **Create practice problems** at varying difficulty levels
4. **Anticipate misconceptions** based on how topics are typically misunderstood

## Sample Prompt

```markdown
I just taught [topic] in my [course name] class. Based on this content, 
what questions are students most likely to ask during office hours? 
For each likely question, suggest 2-3 different ways I might explain 
the concept to students who are struggling.

[Paste lecture notes or key concepts covered]
```
