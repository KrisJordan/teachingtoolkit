# Midterm Check-In

The middle of the semester is an ideal time to assess how things are going and make adjustments. A brief check-in with students can provide valuable insights and demonstrate that you care about their learning experience.

## Quick Survey Questions

Consider asking students:

1. What's helping you learn in this class?
2. What's making learning difficult?
3. What's one thing you wish I knew about your experience in this course?
4. What's one thing you could do differently to improve your learning?
