# End of Semester Checklist

A comprehensive checklist to close out the semester smoothly and meet all deadlines.

---

## :material-calendar-week: Last Week of Classes

### Final Exam Preparation

- [ ] Confirm final exam date, time, and location (check for room changes!)
- [ ] Send detailed final exam announcement to students
- [ ] Schedule accommodated testing with ARS
- [ ] Hold review session or office hours
- [ ] Finalize and print/prepare final exam

### Wrapping Up Content

- [ ] Complete any remaining course content
- [ ] Tie course themes together
- [ ] Preview how skills/knowledge will be useful going forward
- [ ] Thank students for their engagement

### Course Evaluations

- [ ] Remind students to complete course evaluations
- [ ] Consider giving class time for evaluation completion
- [ ] Explain how you use feedback to improve

---

## :material-calendar-check: Finals Week

### Grading

- [ ] Clear out any remaining ungraded work
- [ ] Grade final assignments and exams promptly
- [ ] Calculate final grades according to syllabus
- [ ] Double-check calculations, especially borderline cases
- [ ] Document any grade adjustments or curving applied

### Office Hours

- [ ] Hold extended office hours during finals
- [ ] Be available for last-minute questions
- [ ] Prepare for student concerns about grades

---

## :material-send: Grade Submission

!!! warning "Don't miss the deadline!"
    Grade submission deadlines are firm. Check the [Academic Calendar](https://registrar.unc.edu/academic-calendar/) for exact dates.

- [ ] Review ConnectCarolina grade submission procedures
- [ ] Submit grades by the deadline
- [ ] Handle any incomplete (IN) grade contracts if needed
- [ ] Verify grades were submitted successfully
- [ ] Save records of your grade calculations

---

## :material-archive: Archiving & Records

### Course Materials

- [ ] Archive your Canvas course (it will eventually become read-only)
- [ ] Save copies of materials you may want to reuse or modify
- [ ] Note any copyrighted materials that need updating next time
- [ ] Back up any content not stored in Canvas

### Records Retention

- [ ] Save grade records securely
- [ ] Retain exams and major assignments per department policy
- [ ] Document any academic integrity cases and outcomes
- [ ] Save any student work that may be used as examples (with permission)

---

## :material-head-lightbulb: Quick Reflection

Before you step away, jot down quick notes while things are fresh:

- What worked really well this semester?
- What would you definitely change next time?
- Were there any resources or tools you wish you'd had?
- What surprised you about student learning this semester?

See [Reflecting & Iterating](reflection.md) for a deeper reflection framework.
