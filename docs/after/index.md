## End of Semester Timeline

<div class="timeline-item" markdown>
### Last Week of Classes

- Communicate final exam details
- Provide study guidance and review materials
- Complete any remaining grading from the semester
- Send course evaluation reminders
</div>

<div class="timeline-item" markdown>
### Finals Week

- Administer final exam
- Hold extended office hours for questions
- Begin final grading
</div>

<div class="timeline-item" markdown>
### After Finals

- Complete all grading by the deadline
- Submit grades through ConnectCarolina
- Review course evaluations when available
- Document what worked and what to change
</div>
