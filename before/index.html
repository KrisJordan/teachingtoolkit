    Before the Semester¶

    The weeks before a semester begins are crucial for setting yourself—and your students—up for success. This section contains resources to help you prepare thoughtfully and efficiently.

    In This Section¶

    ### Semester Kickoff Checklist The essential tasks to complete before day one

    ### Course Design Essentials Foundations for effective course planning

    ### Setting Up Canvas Configure your course site for student success

    Timeline Overview¶

    4-6 Weeks Before¶

    • Review and update syllabus
    • Order textbooks and materials
    • Request any needed classroom technology
    • Plan major assignments and due dates

    2-3 Weeks Before¶

    • Set up Canvas course site
    • Create first week's content and activities
    • Prepare welcome announcement
    • Test any technology you'll be using

    Week Before¶

    • Publish Canvas course to students
    • Send welcome email/announcement
    • Verify classroom access and setup
    • Review roster and note any accommodations
