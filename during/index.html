    Common Mid-Semester Challenges¶

    Students aren't reading announcements

    Try the TL;DR approach: Put the most critical information in a brief bullet list at the top. Save context and details for below the fold. See our Announcements Checklist for templates.

    Attendance or engagement is dropping

    Mid-semester slump is real. Consider a brief anonymous survey, a class discussion about what's working, or introducing a new activity type. Our Midterm Check-In guide can help.

    I'm falling behind on grading

    You're not alone. Consider whether you can provide meaningful feedback faster (rubrics, audio comments), or whether some assignments could be completion-graded. Talk to colleagues about sustainable practices.
