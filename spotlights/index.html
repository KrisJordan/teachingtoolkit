    Dr. Sarah Chen¶

    Department of Economics | Teaching since 2015

    "The best class sessions are the ones where I talk the least."

    Dr. Chen transformed her large-enrollment economics course with active learning techniques. Her students now spend more time problem-solving and less time passively listening—and they're performing better on assessments.
